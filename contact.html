Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard orders are dispatched within 2-3 weeks. Delivery to the UK takes 5-7 business days after dispatch. Custom or made-to-order pieces typically take 4-6 weeks depending on embroidery complexity.

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unworn items in original condition. Made-to-order and custom pieces are non-returnable. Please contact hello@meuou.com to initiate a return.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship to the UK, Europe, and selected countries worldwide. International shipping costs and timeframes vary by location. Contact us for a shipping quote to your country.

How do I care for my Meuou piece?

Most pieces are dry-clean only to preserve intricate embroidery and fabrics. Each item includes detailed care instructions. For hand-embroidered pieces, we recommend professional dry cleaning at a specialist facility.

Can I get a custom size or design?

Yes! We offer custom tailoring and bespoke designs. Please email wholesale@meuou.com with your requirements, and our team will provide a quote and timeline for your custom piece.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! Luxury gift wrapping is available upon request at checkout. Each order comes beautifully packaged in our branded boxes.

How are your pieces made?

Every Meuou piece is handcrafted in Pakistan by skilled artisans. From hand embroidery to precise tailoring, each garment is created with traditional techniques and modern design sensibilities.

What about sizing?

We offer sizes XS to XL. Detailed size charts are available on each product page. For made-to-order pieces, we can accommodate custom measurements. Please email us your measurements for guidance.

Are your pieces sustainable?

We're committed to ethical production. All our pieces are made in Pakistan with fair labor practices, premium natural materials, and minimal waste. We also plant a tree for every order placed.

Can I purchase for corporate events or bulk orders?

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders and corporate gifting. For orders of 5+ items, please contact wholesale@meuou.com for a custom quote.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your parcel in real-time through our shipping partner's website.
